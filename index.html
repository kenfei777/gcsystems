What Makes Us Different

Genetics, craft standards, and flexible allocation

1

In-House Genetic Capability

Genetics are owned and managed within our production platform. Our breeding and seed development program in Canada has operated under long-standing leadership with decades of cultivation and genetic experience.

We treat genetics as a long-term production asset — not a short-term strain rotation.

2

Controlled Cultivation & Craft Standards

Cultivation direction remains under experienced technical leadership, ensuring continuity in genetic expression and production discipline across cycles.

  • Slow hang-dried
  • Hand-trimmed
  • Slow-cured under defined parameters
  • Moisture-controlled for stability and terpene integrity
  • Allocated on fresh production cycles
3

Flexible Allocation

New supply relationships often begin with modest volumes. We are comfortable starting with trial shipments, initial portfolio placements, gradual scale-up, and structured longer-term supply.

Our goal is simple: Make it easy to start — and easy to scale if the fit is right.
