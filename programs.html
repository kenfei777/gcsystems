Programs

Flexible Portfolio Structure

Our portfolio is structured around selected, market-aligned cultivars developed specifically for Australian medical demand. In addition to the listed selections, we maintain access to a broader stabilised cultivar base and can adjust production choices as partner requirements evolve.

Partners may access

  • Selected cultivars curated for the Australian market
  • Additional profiles available within our active production program
  • Tailored selections where specific requirements arise

We view our catalogue as a working framework — not a limitation.

Flower Program

Medicinal cannabis flower remains the primary format in Australia. Our Flower Program focuses on:

  • Repeatable cultivar performance
  • Controlled post-harvest standards
  • Fresh-cycle allocation
  • Flexible order sizing

We prioritise product integrity and dependable performance over rapid SKU turnover.
